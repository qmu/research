# Image generation, concise overviews, and the dated survey-article series

## Summary

Adds an image-generation benchmark topic, makes every published article lead with a concise results overview, and restructures the publishing model into a dated survey-article series: each topic's current page carries its own trend chart and links to all past surveys, with the Japanese page always a translation of the English current page. Fixes the reported English/Japanese structural inconsistency at its root.

## Key Changes

- New published topic: image-generation benchmark (latency, per-image cost, rubric-checked prompt adherence, exact-text rendering; fixed vision-judge, fixture path keyless)
- Every published article's §4 is a concise best/median/worst overview; per-subject tables moved to §7, guarded by a §4 compactness budget
- Dated survey-article series (ADR 0006, supersedes 0005): current page = latest survey's 7-section article + 推移 (trend) block + 過去の調査 (past surveys) links
- English → translate → Japanese enforced by the real-run pipeline (compose then translate the current page), so the two languages cannot fork
- Every dated survey is mirrored to qmu-co-jp (Japanese frames to the Japanese section, English frames to the English section) with language-matched past-survey links, so they resolve in both sections
- Trend point extractors added for OCR, image-generation, and RAG
